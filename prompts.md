# Prompt Engineering - Task 1

## Prompt 1: Homepage

You are an expert website copywriter.

Create homepage content for a beauty salon called Bloom Beauty Salon located in Hyderabad.

Include:
1. Hero Heading
2. Hero Subheading
3. About Us
4. Why Choose Us
5. Five Benefits
6. One Customer Testimonial
7. Contact Information

Use simple, friendly, and professional language.

---

## Prompt 2: Services

Create detailed service page content for Bloom Beauty Salon.

Include:
- Haircuts
- Hair Coloring
- Bridal Makeup
- Facials
- Spa Treatments

Write around 80–100 words for each service in a professional and customer-friendly tone.

---

## Prompt 3: Call To Action

Create persuasive Call-To-Action sections for Bloom Beauty Salon.

Include:
- Book Appointment
- Call Now
- Visit Our Salon
- First Visit Discount

Use friendly marketing language.
